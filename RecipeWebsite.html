Gullab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil to create the syrup
  2. in a mixing bowl , combine flour, paneer, sooji, baking powder and baking soda. mix them and make a dough
  3. divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them in to round balls
  4. heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn golden brown
  5. transfer the fried gulab Jamun into cooled sugar syrup
  6. enjoy your homemade GULLAB JAMUN !!!!
